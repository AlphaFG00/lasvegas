Nove Italiano

This expensive sister to N9ne sits atop the Palms' super-luxury Fantasy Tower and boasts one of the best views in town, along with beautiful modern decor. The steaks are mediocre and the service is steeped with attitude. But for all that, the only complaint you'll hear about the Nove spaghetti, prepared with lobster, shrimp, crab, scallop, calamari and basil, will relate to its $43 price tag.

Open: 5:30- 11:00PM

Close Sunday and Monday

Joe's Seafood Prime Steak and Stone Crab

Fans of this Miami-spawned "favorite" inside the Forum Shops at Caesars claim it's "better than the original" thanks to an "extensive, diverse" menu packed with "incredibly fresh" seafood (including "amazing" stone crabs), "perfectly cooked" steaks and "to-die-for" desserts; with a "professional yet amiable" staff providing "five-star" service in a "traditional" room that recalls "old Vegas", it's a "special treat" that's "well worth" the "expensive" cost.

Open: 11:30AM – 10:30PM

STK Las Vegas

There's "never a dull moment" at this direct-from-NYC steakhouse in the Cosmopolitan, where the "delish" (though "premium-priced") eats go toe-to-toe with the "hip", "sexy" nightclub vibe; solid service is another plus, but "forget about any dinner conversation" – "loud music" is part of the experience.

Open 6:00 – 12:00PM

MASTRO`S OCEAN CLUB,

Delicious seafood", "awesome steaks" and "innovative cocktails" draw diners to the "spectacular" "tree-house" setting of this "upscale" chain outpost perched in an "overhanging cage" at Crystals at CityCenter; an "extremely attentive", "professional" staff ensures the service is as "top-shelf" as the food, no wonder fans agree the "wonderful dining experience" warrants the "breathtakingly expensive" prices.

Located in: The Shops at Crystals

Address: The Shops at Crystals, 3720 S Las Vegas Blvd, Las Vegas, NV 89109

Menu: urbanspoon.com


Reservations: opentable

Morton's Steakhouse

Less than a mile away from the Las Vegas Strip surrounded by the glitz and glamour of top casinos and entertainment venues, Morton's Steakhouse is located at the corner of Paradise and Flamingo Roads. Private boardrooms are available for breakfast, lunch and dinner.


Monday – Thursday 5pm – 11pm

Friday – Saturday 4pm – 11pm Sunday 5pm – 10pm

Maggianos Little Italy

Our goal at Maggiano's Little Italy® is to bring the inviting feeling of the old neighborhood a little closer to your neighborhood. To be more than just a place to eat, but rather, be a gathering place where you and your friends and family can slow down, enjoy a great meal and reconnect. Located on the second level of the Fashion Show Mall on Las Vegas Boulevard across from Wynn Las Vegas.


Open daily from 11:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m.

TKOI Las Vegas

Koi Las Vegas, one of the hottest spots on the LA and New York dining scenes, has arrived at the Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino. A celebrity favorite, Koi has received praise for its Japanese-inspired cuisine with California accents. Koi's menu features a variety of savory cooked dishes, traditional sushi and sashimi, as well as creative rolls. The restaurant is wrapped in a seductive harmony of Asian design elements, combining an exotic Eastern aura with a genuine sense of Hollywood energy and style.


Sunday – Thursday 6:00 p.m. – 10:30 p.m

Friday and Saturday 6:00 p.m. – 11:30 p.m.

Rio Carnival World Buffet

Recognized as the best buffet in Vegas year after year by guests and critics alike, the Carnival World Buffet has raised the bar in buffet dining once again, offering more variety in food choices (more than 300 dishes prepared fresh daily) and a new contemporary design that make the best buffet in Vegas even better.


Breakfast $14.99 plus tax, 7am-11am Mon-Fri, 7:30am – 10:30am Sat-Sun

Lunch $16.99 plus tax, 11am-3:30pm Mon-Fri

Dinner $23.99 plus tax, 3:30pm-10pm Nightly
